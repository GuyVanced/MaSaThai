**1. In which year did Malaysia gain independence?**

- A) 1947
- B) 1957
- C) 1963
- D) 1971

**Answer : C (1963)** 

**2. What is the capital city of Singapore?**

- A) Kuala Lumpur
- B) Bangkok
- C) Singapore City
- D) Jakarta
  
**Answer : C (Singapore City)**

**3. In which city are the Petronas Towers, the tallest twin towers in the world, located?**
